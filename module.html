The Deeptech Founder’s Blueprint

From Moonshot to Market

Deeptech is not SaaS. You cannot iterate your way out of physics violations or regulatory dead-ends. This is your framework to bridge radical innovation and commercial reality.

Workshop Module Structure

Module 1 09:00 – 09:30

The Bifocal Strategy

Defining the Moonshot Thesis & Near-Term Product Roadmap

Learn to align a massive geopolitical or economic thesis (The Moonshot) with immediate, revenue-generating products. Survive the "Valley of Death" while aiming for the stars.

Module 2 09:30 – 10:15

Deeptech Systems Thinking

Designing for Complexity: Tech Moats, Regulations, and First-Principles Economics

Stop viewing tech, regulation, and economics in silos. We use First Principles Analysis to validate techno-economic feasibility and construct a defensible Tech Moat from Day 0.

Module 3 10:15 – 11:00

Strategic Engineering

Mission Objectives, Requirements, and Smart MVP Prioritization

With limited funding, what do you build first? Learn to prioritize critical MVP subsystems ensuring you hit milestones that unlock the next round of funding.

Hands-On 11:00 – 12:00

The Deeptech Canvas Sprint

Case Study Analysis & Immediate Framework Application

Use our digital canvas tools to apply the framework to your specific idea in real-time. Deconstruct a real-world success/failure.

Who This Is For

  • Aspiring Deeptech Founders: Structure your vision before you burn your first dollar.
  • Academic Researchers: Translate your lab R&D into a viable commercial vehicle.
  • Innovation Strategists: Learn how to assess and nurture frontier technologies.
  • Non-Tech Founders: Gain the literacy to lead and manage high-tech ventures.

The Experience

  • The Framework: Master a proprietary structure for deeptech company building.
  • The Case Study: Deconstruct a real-world deeptech success/failure.
  • The Application: Use digital canvas tools to apply the framework to your idea.
  • The Network: Connect with a curated group of peers building the future.
